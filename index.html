Imagine your dog has been eating the same kibble for a few years, then suddenly the food goes out of stock and you need to find an alternative. Or maybe you regularly mix up your dog's food for a bit of variety and to balance the nutrients.

Whatever the reason, if you're introducing a new food to your dog, it's best to do it gradually by slowly decreasing the old food and increasing the new. Many people recommend transitiong over the course of a week, but some dogs will only need a few days, and others up to a month.
